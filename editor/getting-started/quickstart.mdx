---
title: "Quickstart"
description: "Get started with Squeditor!"
---

Follow these steps to get started with Squeditor.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose a plan">
    Go to [squeditor.com](https://squeditor.com) and choose a plan that fit your needs to create an account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a new project">
    Once you're logged in, click on the "Create New Project" button in the [dashboard](https://squeditor.com/dashboard) and choose a template to start with.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start editing">
    After project creation, you'll be redirected to the editor where you can start editing or customizing your website.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export or Deploy">
    Once you're done editing, you can export your website as a static HTML website or deploy it to your hosting provider.
  </Step>
</Steps>
